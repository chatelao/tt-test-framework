## Input/Output Definitions

| Signal | Type | Width |
|--------|------|-------|
| UI_IN | input | 8 |
| UO_OUT | output | 8 |

## First 10 Cycles

| Cycle | Phase | UI_IN | UO_OUT |
|-------|-------|-------|-------|
| 0 | Both Low (IN4=0, IN6=0) | 0x0 | 0x0 |
| 1 | One High (IN4=1, IN6=0) | 0x10 | 0x0 |
| 2 | Other High (IN4=0, IN6=1) | 0x40 | 0x0 |
| 3 | Both High (IN4=1, IN6=1) | 0x50 | 0x0 |
