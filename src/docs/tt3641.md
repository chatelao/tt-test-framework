## First 10 Cycles

| Cycle | Phase | ui_in | uo_out | uio_in | uio_out | rst_n |
|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|
| 0 | Reset | 0x0 | 0x0 | 0x0 (CMD_EN=0, REG_SEL[0]=0, RW=0, REG_SEL[1]=0) | 0x0 | 0x0 |
| 1 | Load A = 10 (Byte 0) | 0xa | 0x0 | 0x0 (CMD_EN=0, REG_SEL[0]=0, RW=0, REG_SEL[1]=0) | 0x0 | 0x1 |
| 2 | Load A = 10 (Byte 1-3) | 0x0 | 0x0 | 0x0 (CMD_EN=0, REG_SEL[0]=0, RW=0, REG_SEL[1]=0) | 0x0 | 0x1 |
| 3 | Load A = 10 (Byte 1-3) | 0x0 | 0x0 | 0x0 (CMD_EN=0, REG_SEL[0]=0, RW=0, REG_SEL[1]=0) | 0x0 | 0x1 |
| 4 | Load A = 10 (Byte 1-3) | 0x0 | 0x0 | 0x0 (CMD_EN=0, REG_SEL[0]=0, RW=0, REG_SEL[1]=0) | 0x0 | 0x1 |
| 5 | Load B = 20 (Byte 0) | 0x14 | 0x0 | 0x2 (CMD_EN=0, REG_SEL[0]=1, RW=0, REG_SEL[1]=0) | 0x0 | 0x1 |
| 6 | Load B = 20 (Byte 1-3) | 0x0 | 0x0 | 0x2 (CMD_EN=0, REG_SEL[0]=1, RW=0, REG_SEL[1]=0) | 0x0 | 0x1 |
| 7 | Load B = 20 (Byte 1-3) | 0x0 | 0x0 | 0x2 (CMD_EN=0, REG_SEL[0]=1, RW=0, REG_SEL[1]=0) | 0x0 | 0x1 |
| 8 | Load B = 20 (Byte 1-3) | 0x0 | 0x0 | 0x2 (CMD_EN=0, REG_SEL[0]=1, RW=0, REG_SEL[1]=0) | 0x0 | 0x1 |
| 9 | Execute ADD (A = A + B = 10 + 20 = 30) | 0x1 | 0x0 | 0x1 (CMD_EN=1, REG_SEL[0]=0, RW=0, REG_SEL[1]=0) | 0x0 | 0x1 |
