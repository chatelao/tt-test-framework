## Input/Output Definitions

| Signal | Type | Width |
|--------|------|-------|
| ui[0:3] | input | 4 |
| ui[4] | input | 1 |
| ui[5] | input | 1 |
| uo[4] | output | 1 |
| uo[5] | output | 1 |
| uo[0:3] | output | 4 |

## First 10 Cycles

| Cycle | Phase | ui[0:3] | ui[4] | ui[5] | uo[4] | uo[5] | uo[0:3] |
|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|
| 0 | Reset | 0x0 | 0x0 | 0x0 | 0x0 | 0x0 | 0x0 |
| 1 | Request Read Addr 0 | 0x0 | 0x1 | 0x0 | 0x0 | 0x0 | 0x0 |
| 2 | Wait for ready | 0x0 | 0x1 | 0x0 | 0x1 | 0x0 | 0x0 |
| 3 | Wait for response | 0x0 | 0x1 | 0x0 | 0x1 | 0x1 | 0x0 |
| 4 | Wait for response | 0x0 | 0x1 | 0x0 | 0x1 | 0x1 | 0x0 |
