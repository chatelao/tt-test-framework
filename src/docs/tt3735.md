## Input/Output Definitions

| Signal | Type | Width |
|--------|------|-------|
| ENA | input | 1 |
| RST_N | input | 1 |
| UI_IN | input | 8 |
| UO_OUT | output | 8 |

## First 10 Cycles

| Cycle | Phase | ENA | RST_N | UI_IN | UO_OUT |
|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|
| 0 | Reset | 0x1 | 0x0 | 0x0 | 0x0 |
| 1 | AND logic 00 | 0x1 | 0x1 | 0x0 | 0x0 |
| 2 | AND logic 11 | 0x1 | 0x1 | 0x3 | 0x1 |
| 3 | Pass-through | 0x1 | 0x1 | 0xfc | 0xfc |
