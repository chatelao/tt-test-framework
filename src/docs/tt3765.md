## Input/Output Definitions

| Signal | Type | Width |
|--------|------|-------|
| ENA | input | 1 |
| RST_N | input | 1 |
| UI_IN | input | 8 |
| UO_OUT | output | 8 |
| UIO | inout | 8 |

## First 10 Cycles

| Cycle | Phase | ENA | RST_N | UI_IN | UO_OUT | UIO |
|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|
| 0 | Reset | 0x1 | 0x0 | 0x0 | 0x0 | 0x4 |
| 1 | Initialize Mean | 0x1 | 0x1 | 0x64 | 0x0 | 0x3 |
| 2 | Steady State | 0x1 | 0x1 | 0x64 | 0x0 | 0x1 |
