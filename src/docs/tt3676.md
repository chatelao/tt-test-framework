## Input/Output Definitions

| Signal | Type | Width |
|--------|------|-------|
| uo_out | output | 8 |

## First 10 Cycles

| Cycle | Phase | uo_out | rst_n |
|-------|-------|-------|-------|
| 0 | Reset | 0x0 (R0=0, G0=0, B0=0, VSync=0, R1=0, G1=0, B1=0, HSync=0) | 0x0 |
| 1 | Count 1 | 0x8 (R0=0, G0=0, B0=0, VSync=1, R1=0, G1=0, B1=0, HSync=0) | 0x1 |
| 2 | Count 2 | 0x4 (R0=0, G0=0, B0=1, VSync=0, R1=0, G1=0, B1=0, HSync=0) | 0x1 |
| 3 | Count 3 | 0xc (R0=0, G0=0, B0=1, VSync=1, R1=0, G1=0, B1=0, HSync=0) | 0x1 |
